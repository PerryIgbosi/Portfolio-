My Projects

Project 1: Fasten 4

I worked with a team to develop a Connect 4 game using Java. Our goal was to recreate the classic game with a clean interface and smooth gameplay. We successfully built a fully functional version, complete with win detection, animations, and a credits screen. The project strengthened my Java, teamwork, and problem-solving skills

Project 2: Business Idea

During my time at the American University of Nigeria, my team developed a business idea focused on helping individuals set better prices for their products. The project explored how psychological pricing strategies—such as anchoring, decoy pricing, and loss aversion—influence consumer decisions and revenue. We created a concept that could potentially help small businesses make smarter pricing choices.
